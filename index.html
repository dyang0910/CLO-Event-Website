About CLO

  • Location: The Cornell Lab of Ornithology is a member-supported unit of Cornell University in Ithaca, New York.
  • History: Founded by Arthur A. Allen, the Lab was established at Cornell University in 1915.
  • Mission: interpreting and conserving the Earth's biological diversity through research, education, and citizen science focused on birds.
